Overview

JoyAhead ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how our app operates in relation to your data.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not show ads and we do not use analytics or tracking tools. No information is transmitted to our servers or to any third parties.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (such as name, email address, phone number)

  • Diagnostics or crash logs sent off the device

The app may perform essential, on-device processing required for its core functionality. Such processing stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Children’s Privacy

Our app can be used by individuals of all ages, including children, because it does not collect or store personal data. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us by any means outside the app, please contact us and we will handle your request promptly.

Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not share any data with third parties. We do not transfer data internationally because no data is collected or transmitted off your device.

Your Rights

Since we do not process personal data, typical privacy rights (such as access, deletion, or opt-out requests) generally do not apply. If you have any questions, you can always contact us using the details below.
